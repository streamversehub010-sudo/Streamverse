Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information StreamVerse collects when you use this site, and how it is used. StreamVerse is a demo streaming catalog intended to provide information and access to legally authorized content only.

Information we collect

When you create an account, we store your username, email address, and account role. We keep a basic activity log (logins, content changes, moderation actions) for site administration. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

How we use it

Account information is used solely to authenticate you, personalize your experience, and let administrators manage the catalog and viewer accounts. Search terms and browsing activity are not shared outside StreamVerse.

Cookies & local storage

StreamVerse uses your browser's local session data to keep you signed in and to remember basic preferences. You can clear this at any time through your browser settings.

Your choices

You may request that your account and associated data be deleted at any time by contacting us — see the Contact Us page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through our Contact Us page.
